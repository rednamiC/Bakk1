\ac{GENSO}'s aim is to provide a transmission network enabling a \ac{MCC} to communicate with their respective educational satellites the whole day via \acp{GCC} located around the globe. This would prevent \acp{GCC} all over the world to reside unused for the major time of the day as normally every CubeSat sends to only one station. 
\newline
Moreover, as the building of new educational satellites gets cheaper, more and more Universities will start their own "Space Program". These facilities will also be able to profit from the network as they don't neccessarily have to build a new \ac{GCC} to gather the satellite's data.
\newline
To restrict the access to the satellites' data, each \ac{GCC} has to register to an \ac{AUS}.
